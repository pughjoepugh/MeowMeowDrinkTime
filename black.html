Japanese Kitten Valkyrie:

A very personalized martini. Blended gins, blended vermouthes, hint of salt,
and bergamot bitters in the place of a zest

Siamese:

An attempt to excite the palette. White wine brings some fruit and acidity.
Salers from some bitterness and vegetal notes. Apaluz is a mildly smoky mezcal
that brings minerality. Garnished with a oily kalamata olive stuffed with a funky bleu cheese

Meow Meow Drink Time:

Our namesake is a coconut milk clarified cocktail
This cocktail is the love child of a 'last word' and a 'corn and oil'
Fun, silky, wild yet restrained. This is an indoor out cat.

Polka Dot Tiger:

We wanted to make a mamajuana hurricane. Mamajuana is like inverted sangria that is aged on wood
which makes it a drinking bitters. Our batch was not started in time for this testing popup,
this cocktail is still evolving.

Scaredy Cat:

Originally named 'I am not a negroni'. Using the classic combination of gin, campari,
and sweet vermouth just in a very different ratio. Scaredy Cat drinks like a fruitier old fashioned,
or a timid manhattan.

Fancy Feast:

Boilermakers are like ready-to-eat meals. The blend of becherovka and batavia arrack is fun!
Amaro Nonino has stepped in since becherovka is back-ordered. The ratio is no longer 50:50.
Nonino is sweeter and more complex than becherovka.
Paired with a pony of High Life for some easy drinking.
